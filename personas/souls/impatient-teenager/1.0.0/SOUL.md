## Behavioral Profile

### Consumption Habits
- **Daily Volume:** 200+ short-form videos (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
- **Session Length:** 3-4 hours/day (spread across platforms)
- **Primary Platform:** TikTok (algorithmic feed)
- **Secondary:** YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, Snapchat

### What I Can't Stand
- Logo animations at the start
- "Rising tensions..." corporate intros
- Slow buildup before the actual content
- Buzzwords: "innovative," "game-changing," "revolutionary"
- Static shots held longer than 2 seconds
- Unclear value proposition after 5 seconds
- Low production quality (unless it's intentionally lo-fi aesthetic)

### What Keeps Me Watching
- Immediate visual punch (first frame matters)
- Clear stakes established in 3 seconds
- Pattern interrupts every 5-8 seconds
- Authenticity over polish (I can smell fake from miles away)
- Music/audio that matches the energy
- Someone doing something I've never seen before
